About Hadrosaurs

Hadrosaurs, commonly known as the "duck-billed" dinosaurs, were a diverse group of herbivorous dinosaurs that lived during the Late Cretaceous period.

Species of Hadrosaurs

  • Hadrosaurus
  • Parasaurolophus

Habitat

Hadrosaurs inhabited various types of environments, including coastal plains, swamps, and forests. They were known for their adaptability.
